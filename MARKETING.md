# 🎯 Google Ads Strategy for The Pacific

Since I've built your website to be high-converting, here is how you should set up your Google Ads to get those **5 orders tomorrow**.

## 1. Keywords to Bid On
Target "High Intent" keywords. These are people ready to buy *now*.
- `CCTV installation near me`
- `Best home security cameras [Your City Name]`
- `Business surveillance systems`
- `The Pacific CCTV` (for brand protection)

## 2. Setting Up Your Ad (The Apple Style)
**Headline 1:** The Pacific | Vision. Reimagined.
**Headline 2:** #1 CCTV Installation in [City]
**Headline 3:** Book Free Site Survey Today
**Description:** The most advanced security system ever. Pure, powerful, and private. 4K clarity with 24/7 AI-powered alerts. 

## 3. How I've Optimized the Landing Page for Ads
- **Fast Load Speed:** Essential for Google Ads Quality Score (Reduces cost per click).
- **Clear CTA:** The "Buy Now" and "Order Now" buttons are prominent.
- **WhatsApp Integration:** High-converting for mobile users (60-80% of your traffic).
- **SEO & Structure:** Metadata and JSON-LD are ready for Google's crawlers.

## 4. Setting up Conversion Tracking
Once you create your Google Ads account:
1. Go to **Tools and Settings > Conversions**.
2. Create a "Website" conversion for **Form Submissions**.
3. Use the **Google Tag** - I can add the code to `layout.tsx` for you once you have it!
